Day activities

Are you ready to experience the real Belgium? Are you ready to be indulged?
We present you day activities that are:

  • Unique: Experience activities exclusively created for the EUCO!
  • Intimate: Small groups ensure close participation and the ideal opportunity to meet new people!
  • Authentic: Think Belgian! Chocolate, Beer, Diamonds, Printing art design, Fashion, …!

A DIAMOND TOUR

Activity:
If you say diamonds, we say Antwerp! Our city is the world number one in the diamond trade ever since 1447! Today, 84% of all diamonds travels through Antwerp at least once. Have you always wondered how they make diamonds shine? Thanks to the Antwerp World Diamond Centre, you get the chance to experience an exclusive tour behind the Antwerp’s diamond scene! You will visit the diamond fair, a diamond cutter and the diamond laboratory. Only 40 spots for this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity available: first come, first serve! It is so exclusive that we even have to ask for your passport details

Price?
70 EUR

When?
Thursday 5th of April: morning 10 o’clock
Friday 6th of April: morning 10 o’clock

Who?
20 lucky People / tour

How long?
2 hours

PRESS & PRINT

Activity:
Do you think printing is boring? We will proof you wrong! This activity combines a visit to the famous Plantin Moretus museum, a Unesco World Heritage Site, and your own printing workshop! We go back to the 16th century basics.
You can choose between two workshops:
"typeset, cast & print" where you make up a complete page by arranging letters in mirror image in a composing stick and print it on a genuine press.
Or you can do a ‘bookbinding’ workshop where you bookbind your own little notebook, diary or photo album.

(visit museum + workshop)

Evening activities

WEDNESDAY: Fluo-Elrow-Bash

On Wednesday we will break the house down for the first time. Come and join us on an epic evening and discover the magical world of Elrow. On this evening colour, inflatables, confetti, fluo and a lot of good party beats will take you to a higher place. Tonight, Antwerp will overwhelm you with Elrow magic!

What to wear?

Everything is possible during our magical Elrow party. Wear the craziest costumes, wigs, crazy sunglasses, glitter, bring inflatables and finally add a touch of fluo to your outfit. On this evening the sky is the limit!

Location

  • Ikon
  • Straatsburgdok Noordkaai 3
  • 2030 Antwerp
