Day activities

D.I.A.M.O.N.D.
(DISCOVER, ILLUMINATE, ASTONISCH, MARVEL, OVERWHELM, NURTURE, DAZZLE)

Activity:
If you say diamonds, we say Antwerp! Our city is the world number one in the diamond trade ever since 1447! Today, 84% of all diamonds travels through Antwerp at least once. Have you always wondered how they make diamonds shine? Thanks to the Antwerp World Diamond Centre, you get the chance to experience an exclusive tour behind the Antwerp’s diamond scene! You will visit the diamond fair, a diamond cutter and the diamond laboratory. Only 40 spots for this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity available: first come, first serve! It is so exclusive that we even have to ask for your passport details.

Price?
70 EUR

When?
Thursday 5th of April: morning 10 o’clock
Friday 6th of April: morning 10 o’clock

Who?
Only 20 lucky Rotaracters / tour

How long?
2 hours

C.H.O.C.O.
(CREATE, HARVEST, OBSERVE, CONSUME, OBTAIN)

Activity:
Did you know that Belgian chocolate has more cacao than the European average chocolate?! And… that our chocolate industry is since 1910 one of the best in the world thanks to brilliant production standards?! Smell, taste and have fun with chocolate together with 19 other Rotaracters! Create your own chocolate ‘pralines’ and chocolate attributes and decorations during a 3-hour workshop! Yes, of course you can take your masterpiece with you!

Price?
45 EUR (workshop + chocolate)

When?
Thursday 5th of April: noon or afternoon
Saturday 7th of April: morning or noon

Who?
Maximum 20 people/workshop
First come, first served!

How long?
3 hours

P.R.E.S.S.
(PRINT, REBUILD, ENGRAVE, SKETCH, SCRIBBLE)

Activity:
Do you think printing is boring? We will proof you wrong! This activity combines a visit to the famous Plantin Moretus museum, a Unesco World Heritage Site, and your own printing workshop! We go back to the 16th century basics: arranging letters in mirror image in a composing stick, printing on a genuine press, find out how to make an ‘etching’, work with a zinc plate and ‘bookbind’ your own little notebook, diary or photo album.

Price?
19 EUR (visit museum + workshop)

When?
Thursday 5th of April: morning
Friday 6th of April: morning

Who?
Maximum 12 people/workshop

How long?
2 hours

Evening activities

Don't let our nights be ordinary, but exceptional as ONE big happy Rotaract Family!

Fluo-Elrow-Bash
An experience where spectacle, music, color and happiness, essential elements of elrow’s DNA, get together to create that unique and involving experience that our partypeople get themselves into as soon as they cross the front door regardless of city, country, continent or time of the day… Thanks to all these components elrow mutates, together with all it’s public, into a great family that enjoys this experience up-close and personal, something only achieved thanks to an absolute connection between the show, the artists, performers and public alike. Fun and uninhibited entertainment make elrow a unique and unrepeatable experience. Come and join us!

Food Truck Festival
It's time to get outside and bring the community back together. Bring your appetite for the tastiest food on wheels. These vibrant and edgy food havens serve up delicious food from over the world.

Nautical Marine Party
Ay Matey! Swim over for an epic nautical marine party. You will feel like actually being out on the beach. Daydream about seaside and feel it!

Gala evening

Urban ... new ... green ... industrial ...
Bringing you to a green piece of ground where you feel the diversity of the city of Antwerp, but even the history is our aim for the EUCO gala dinner location of 2018. Be prepared for an unforgettable evening with a great menu, extensive choice of drinks, best catering service, vibrant music, ... all with a Belgian "celebration" touch! Stay tuned for more info!

Conference

Together with the University of Antwerp, the E.R.I.C board will be hosting a conference day filled with interesting speakers, interactive workshops, conference stands and country booths.
