Every.One.Counts.

Within the social divisions defind by E.R.I.C. the organization of EUCO 2018 has elected 4 social causes we will integrate in the EUCO program.

1. HEALTH PROJECT: “HIV ONTRAFELEN”

HIV is still one of the most deadly viruses in the world. The therapy can suppress the virus, but the disease still can not be cured. The research team of Prof. dr. Dr. Linos Vandekerckhove, associated with UZGent and UGent, conducts pioneering research into the cure of HIV.
"With 9 out of 10 HIV positive that take HIV inhibitors every day, we can completely suppress the virus, but the virus remains latent in the body and reappears when the medication is no longer taken, so we focus all of our Attention in the research now on the functional healing of HIV ", says Prof. Dr. Linos Vandekerckhove, coordinator of the HIV clinic at UZ Gent and head of one of the HIV research labs at Ghent University. The Ghent researchers now want to switch off the HIV virus in the first instance in order to achieve a functional cure. This means that they are looking for ways to stop HIV medication with HIV-positive, without the virus returning. Recent research developments suggest that this should be feasible.
Help finding the cure!

WEB: www.hivontrafelen.be

2. PEACE PROJECT: “SHELTERBOX”

Right now, around 85 million people around the world have been made homeless by natural disaster and conflict. We're working to change this.
By providing emergency shelter and tools for families robbed of their homes by disaster, we're transforming despair into hope. We are impatient to see a world where no family is left without shelter. Are you?

WEB: www.shelterbox.org

3. GROWTH PROJECT: “BEDNET”

“Bednet” ensures that sick children and young people who are temporarily unable to attend school are still part of the class from home. That way they join the lesson and they continue to see their friends. Pupils who are absent or absent for a long time due to illness, surgery, accident or pregnancy can follow internet education. Both five-year olds and children from primary and secondary education are eligible.

“Bednet” connects the sick child live via the internet with his or her own class: the class sees the Bednetter on the computer screen in the back of the classroom. Through a camera that the child can control from home, the child can follow the lessons and / or talk with his classmates.

Our non-profit organization always works in consultation with the parents, the school and other organizations such as Temporary Home Education, the volunteers from School & Sickness and the hospital schools.

The advantages
Join the lesson and everything else. Your child is part of the lesson. 9 out of 10 of the Bednetters move on to the next year without doubling.
The child has regular contact with the friends of the class. So it forgets that it is sick and can be back in the classroom, with his friends. He no longer feels alone.
Help young people grow!

WEB: www.bednet.be

4. GREEN PROJECT: “PAKT”

Do you ever think about a vegetables garden on top of your roof? Do you regret to hide away your green fingers because you live in the city? STOP THAT RIGHT NOW!
We will take you to the biggest urban farm in Antwerp. Just on top of Pakt’s roof the is a vegetable garden, a chicken coop, a fish farm and much more. Discover how we can make all the cities greener.

Pakt calls itself: “Hotspot and stew of entrepreneurs and urban farmers who feed the heart of Antwerp with creativity and culinary experiments.”! Guess you all wanna be part of this and want to visit the site of Pakt.
Let’s be green together and make the world a greener place!

WEB: www.pakt-antwerpen.be
