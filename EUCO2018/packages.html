We are super excited to host you during the next European Rotaract Conference in Antwerp, Belgium.
Each year the largest Rotaract even in Europe gathers over a 1.000 enthusiastic and ambitious youngster.
Get a taste of the amazing atmosphere and all the organisation has to offer!
We want to show you our beautiful city, our social commitment and much more! Do you want to be part of EuCo Antwerp 2018?

Some registration tips for on the next registration wave

Our registration team advises you to keep these points in mind when booking your personal package. They will ensure a smooth registration, and they will answer some of your most crucial questions

  1. We advise not to use smartphones to register for your own package. In case you do use a smartphone, it could occur that you will have to scroll to the right or left in order to see all the buttons (e.g. 'next').
  2. When your preferred method of payment does not work correctly, you can always opt for an alternative method such as a regular bank transfer.
  3. Patience you must have my young padawan, do not panic! You have 20 minutes to complete your registration form once you have selected your package. If you still have questions or problems do not hesitate to contact us on our FB page.
  4. DO NOT REFRESH! Once you are in the virtual queue you position is saved within the internet session you are in. Should you refresh, you'll be thrown out of the queue and you will have to restart at the end of the queue.
  5. Book a package. It's a once in a lifetime... Trust me! It's true

Pre EuCo in Ghent - Bruges

  • 1st - 4th April 2018
  • 165

Discover Belgium’s medieval though trending cities Ghent and Bruges, during our Pre EUCO! This journey starts in Bruges historic center, which was granted the UNESCO World Heritage status. Bruges is an active, living city and preserved the architectural and urban structures throughout the years. Good news for all sweet-tooths joining us, as Bruges is also considered to be the Chocolate Capital of the World! With more than 50 chocolatiers you’ll be able to lick your chops at almost every street corner. Film tip, if you’re eager to get a sneak peek: This best-preserved medieval city gained some popularity thanks to the Colin Farrell–led black comedy In Bruges.

After staying one night in the charming city of Bruges, we will continue our visit on the cobblestones of medieval Ghent. In the morning, you will see the sun rising up behind Gravensteen Castle. Ghent breathes history but is also brimming with culture and modern city life. Ghents history, art, wonderful architecture, waters and joie de vivre will win you over for sure! Not convinced yet? Lonely Planet called Ghent Europe’s best kept secret!
Come, see and be impressed.

  • Where: 1 night Bruges Leonardo Hotel *** and 2 nights Ghent Hostel De Draecke
  • Who: Pre EUCO team 2018
  • Why: Historical sights, workshops, local beer and food tastings, transportation, diner, party, …
  • Registrations opens on the Thursday 30th of November 2017 at 19h00 (CET, UTC +1)
  • Sold Out

After 3 days of discovering by experience you will be continuing your visit to the Euco 2018 in Antwerp.

Pre EuCo in Amsterdam

  • 1st - 4th April 2018
  • 165

Amsterdam, they call it the city of sins or Venice of the North. We would like to call it the city of freedom. Before you go to Antwerp, you need to go a realy city; call it Amsterdam!
Why?
Celebrating the Golden anniversary from Rotaract The Netherlands (50 years) and Pearl anniversary from Rotaract Amsterdam (30 years).
Time for some facts about this beautiful city.

165: That’s the number of canals in Amsterdam. In total these waterways add up to more than 100 kilometers or about 60 miles.

3: That’s the number of Xs in Amsterdam’s coat of arms. The XXX on the city’s coat of arms does not stand for pornography, so get your mind out of the gutter. Most historians say they represent crosses of Saint Andrew. St. Andrew was a fisherman who was martyred on an X-shaped cross in the 1st century. Amsterdam was originally a fishing village, so there you have it. Or it could symbolize the three kisses that Dutch people give when greeting. You decide.

1.515: That’s the number of bars and cafes in Amsterdam. Wow! That’s a lot of Heineken and better than the Belgium beers;)

881.000: That’s approximately the number of bicycles in Amsterdam.

Prior to the European Conference Antwerp 2018, you will have the unique opportunity to explore Amsterdam and the Netherlands
in an excellent three-day program. During your stay, you will spend three nights in the 4**** Novotel hotel in the city center including a freshly made breakfast by chefs, 24-hour open gym, sauna and the possibility for a late-night drink in the 15-meter long bar.
On April 1st the PRE EUCO guests arrive, and we will start the evening with an impressive boat party with at least 100 Dutch Rotaractors.
All drinks and snacks included. The sky is the limit!
During the other days, you will get the chance to see the fascinating history of the Golden Age, gorgeous paintings of the worlds most famous painters like Rembrandt and Van Gogh and discover the secrets of the Red Light District. You'll be amazed by all the thousands of cyclists and old windmills!

And of course, we will introduce you to the beautiful tulips in the Keukenhof where we will get a private tour and access to the park.
You'll taste the famous stroopwafels, cheese, licorice and other delicious Dutch snacks.
On April 4th we all travel together to Antwerp by train or bus where lovely hosts will receive us for the EUCO 2018!
Get the Dutch Experience for only €165,-
We hope to welcome you in the beautiful city Amsterdam, the realy city;!
