Packages

Hi there!

We are super excited to host you during the next European Rotaract Conference in Antwerp, Belgium.
Each year the largest Rotaract even in Europe gathers over a 1.000 enthusiastic and ambitious youngster.
Get a taste of the amazing atmosphere and all the organisation has to offer!
We want to show you our beautiful city, our social commitment and much more! Do you want to be part of EuCo Antwerp 2018?
Get yourself a package during one of the registration waves.

Underneath you have an overview of the different packages available to you. Several options are available to you depending on your preferences; do you wish to sleep close to your friends in one of the Conference hostels?
Can't get of work for more then one day? No problem! We got a three-day package available.
Are you a gala-type? No problem! You can choose to join the gala-only. It promises to by a 'shining' evening...

Registrations will open in waves. For now three waves are scheduled. Herewith an overview;

  • First wave [EARLY BIRD] Tuesday the 28th of November 2017 at 19h00 (CET, UTC +1).
    Only a limited amount of Pieter Paul Rubens packages will be made available at reduced rates. This rate is subjected to limited availability. Main hotel - Crowne Plaza hotel - only.
  • Second wave Thursday the 30th of December 2017 at 19h00 (CET, UTC +1).
    All five day packages will be made available. Other packages - gala-only, without accommodation and three-day deals, pre- and post-trips - may be made available.
  • Third wave Thursday the 7th of December 2017 at 19h00 (CET, UTC +1).
    All packages are open for sale. This is the closing sale... If you want to be part of this historic event, then this is your last chance!
IMPORTANT - The European Rotaract Conference in Antwerp, Belgium partnered with a total of three hotels and one hostel.
These accommodations are included in the packages Pieter Paul Rubens and Willem Elsschot.
Please have a look at the different accommodations prior to your bookingon, on this page.

Upon registration you will be asked to select your preferred hotel or hostel. What the difference:

  • Crown Plaza Antwerp; Our main hotel on the outskirts of Antwerp. The city centre is a 10-min trip by public transport. The hotel has a beautiful wellness centre available to our participants.
  • IBIS Antwerp Center and Theater Hotel Antwerp; Our two city hotels are located at the Theater square 'Theaterplein' in the city centre. No wellness, but everything is within walking distance. On Saturday and Sunday the square holds a market; an abundance of local delicacies, fun objects, etc. You can even by birds!!
  • Hostel Pulcinella; Your aim is to explore the city from within, stay close to fellow Rotaractors at the accommodation and do all this as budget-friendly as possible! The hostel is your preferred choice!

Getting started on the registration...
Have you heard of the music festival Tomorrowland? And how the registration each year is a great adventure?
We love to have you all register as smooth as possible and without any frustration. So one laptop is sufficient and one click on the registration button as well! When you are at the order page and you have selected your preferred package, you have a total of 10 minutes to complete your registration form. During this 10 minutes the package is yours! Don't lose that opportunity and cease it with the completion of the registration form!

A bit thirsty already? You could opt to buy some 'booze packages' prior to conference. Tokens will be given upon check-in in your preferred hotel or hostel. These tokens are the only payment options during the conference for drinks and food at all our conference venues.
Please note that tokens are non-refundable. You can find more info here.

Payment via bank transfer is free of charge. Other payment options such as Maestro, VISA, MASTERCARD, American Express, Ideal, etc. are available to you but subjected to an additional fee by the booking system. A service fee of â‚¬1,50 applies to each booking.
